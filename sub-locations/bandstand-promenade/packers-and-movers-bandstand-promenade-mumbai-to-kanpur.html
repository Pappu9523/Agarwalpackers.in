Our dedicated move coordinator will be your one-point-of-contact throughout the process. They will guide you through every step, from providing a free in-home estimate to creating a customized moving plan that caters to your specific needs and budget.

Expert Packing:

Our well-trained packers are experts in using high-quality packing materials to ensure your belongings, from delicate furniture to cherished artifacts, are secured for the journey. We will carefully categorize and label everything, making unpacking at your new Kanpur home a breeze.

Safe and Secure Transportation:

We utilize robust vehicles with advanced GPS tracking systems to ensure the safe and timely delivery of your belongings to Kanpur. Throughout the transportation process, our team will keep you updated on the whereabouts of your shipment.

Comprehensive Insurance:

For added peace of mind, we offer comprehensive insurance coverage to protect your valuables against unforeseen circumstances during transit.

Efficient Unloading and Placement:

Our team will carefully unload your belongings at your new Kanpur residence. We can even assist with furniture placement, ensuring a smooth transition into your new space.

Competitive Rates:

Vehicle Transportation:

Flexible Storage Solutions:

Beyond the Essentials:

  1. Disassembly and Reassembly of Furniture: Our team can expertly disassemble your furniture for safe transport and reassemble it upon arrival at your new home.
