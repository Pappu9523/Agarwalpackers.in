History

1984 - A very humble beginning of DRS Group. The Organization was formed in the year 1984 ( AGARWAL TEMPO TRANSPORT OF INDIA), by Shri Dayanand Agarwal Ji, with one Mahindra vehicle having it's registration number ABT 812. Prior to this, he has worked in Economic Transport Organization and has got rich experience of the transport Industry. During his tenure with ETO, he was in Chennai, Bangalore, Kolkatta and Nepal. Eventually after this he decided to start his own organization in the name of DRS.
