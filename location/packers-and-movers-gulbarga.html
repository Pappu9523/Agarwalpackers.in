All About Kalaburagi (Gulbarga) - Southern State of Karnataka

Gulbarga district, now known as Kalaburagi district is situated in the southern state of Karnataka, India. Serving as the administrative center of the district, Kalaburagi city holds the distinction of being its headquarters. Additionally, the district itself serves as the headquarters of the Kalaburagi division.

The name "Kalaburagi" finds its roots in the Kannada language, meaning "stony land." The district has a rich historical legacy, as it was under the control of the Chalukyas in the 6th century CE. Subsequently, the Rashtrakutas briefly gained control, only to be ousted by the Chalukyas, who reigned for the following two centuries. Eventually, the Kalachuris took over until the 12th century, when they were displaced by the Yadavas.
