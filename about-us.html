Original Agarwal Packers and Movers

The Original Agarwal Packers and Movers is the most prominent name in the logistics industry that strives to offer high-quality logistics and transport solutions to its diverse client roster. With more than 37 years of expertise, Agarwal Packers and Movers has carved out a niche for itself and is known to be one of the best and most reputed logistics companies in India.

With the efforts of Mr. Dayanand Agarwal (Group Chairman & Founder, DRS Group), the network of our company has expanded to 56 branches in 40 cities. We are now covering more than 1600 locations and cater to these areas with the help of more than 600 vehicles.

Mr. Dayanand Agarwal who is the founder of Agarwal Packers and Movers also goes by the moniker of ‘Bade Bhaiya’. Mr. Dayanand has always acted as a pillar of strength and has guided the team to strive for nothing but the best.

To be recognized as a leader and a pioneer in offering world-class logistics services and strengthening the existing leadership position in the logistics and transportation industry, Agarwal Packers and Movers is committed to offering services that achieve utmost customer satisfaction. We strive to deliver top-notch logistics solutions and ease the pain of home relocation by offering a hassle-free and smooth transit experience.

Agarwal Packers and Movers is the original and long-serving shifting company since 1984. To help our clientele differentiate the original from the other players in the market with a similar name, we have also initiated the #BadeBhaiyaMatlab campaign.

The term Bade Bhaiya signifies a dependable, reliable, and respected person. For Agarwal Packers and Movers, Bade Bhaiya is Mr. Dayanand Agarwal.

Our existing clients and our future associations must be able to distinguish between the ‘original’ and others easily. So, the next time you are looking for relocation services, search for Agarwal Packers and Movers’ Bade Bhaiya!

Vision

As India's largest and No.1 relocation company, Agarwal Packers and Movers aim to:

  • Self-assess to improve our services each day.
  • Consistently build and strengthen relationships with new and existing clients.
  • Stay honest and truthful to our ethics, values, and principles.
  • Deliver efficient and competent services to our clients and exceed their expectations.
  • Encourage and inspire our team members to strive for excellence.

Mission

Agarwal Packers and Movers has the mission to be recognized as one of the pioneering forces and leaders in the logistics and transportation industry. For this, we have taken various initiatives like:

Chapp Van

CHAPP VAN is one of a kind, custom-designed van that is exclusively created to cater to the diverse requirements of customers. It enables the movement of the entire household including vehicles, precious artifacts, and even pets and plants with utmost safety, thus eliminating the hassles of availing of multiple services.

Double Home Carrier

With a total capacity of 6 tonnes, the vehicle can accommodate two-bedroom houses, all in one vehicle. The Double home carrier offers an impressive space of 1280 cubic feet. Agarwal Packers and Movers currently boast the strength of 50 Double Home Carriers that enhance our efficiency manifolds.

We are the leader of many products in the logistics industry:

Agarwal Packers and Movers is a pioneer of various products in the logistics industry that have led to the evolution and a lot of developments in this area.

Agarwal Packers and Movers is the:

  • First to come up with the concept of ‘Innovative Chapp Van’ in the country.
  • The pioneering force behind the idea of the ‘Portable Home Concept’ in India.
  • First-ever company to innovate the ‘LCD Box’ in the country.

Our History

Shri Dayanand Agarwal Ji has had a very humble beginning. But with his consistent effort and perseverance, his journey has now become a success story. Ever since the inception of the DRS Group in 1984, Shri Dayanand Agarwal Ji aka Bade Bhaiya has been the driving force behind Agarwal Packers and Movers, formerly known as Agarwal Tempo Transport of India.

Shri Dayanand Agarwal Ji previously worked in the Economic Transport Organization from where he gathered extensive and in-depth experience and knowledge of the transport Industry which facilitated and encouraged him to start his venture under the name of DRS Group.

An organization that once commenced its operation with one Mahindra vehicle now possesses more than 600 vehicles to offer services all across the nation. Read more

Our Professional Packing and Moving Services

  1. Domestic Shifting: Agarwal Packers and Movers ensure proper packing of your furniture, glassware, and other belongings to help you relocate in a hassle-free manner.
  2. Household Shifting: With some of the most innovative concepts like Portable Home and Perfect Box, Agarwal Packers and Movers assures utmost safety and convenience while moving to a new house.
  3. Office Shifting: Agarwal Packers and Movers hold years of expertise in moving valuable office belongings such as file cabinets, desks, chairs, computer equipment, and other high-tech machinery.
  4. Car Shifting: Agarwal Packers and Movers will help in moving your automobile, bike, bicycle, and car in a hassle-free manner.
  5. Corporate Shifting: Whether it is a financial institute or an MNC Company or even a public sector company, Agarwal Packers and Movers has facilitated various corporate client relocations.
  6. International Shifting: With an entire schedule of delivery options offered by Agarwal Packers and Movers for international air freight requirements, you do not have to worry while shifting overseas.
  7. Storage Facility: With the most dependable and well-equipped storage spaces offered by Agarwal Packers and Movers, you can be sure that your belongings are safe.
  8. Bulk/Commercial Movements: Bulk and commercial movements are a cakewalk for Agarwal Packers and Movers that are served by a strong network of 100 locations offices and service centers catering to 1200 destinations through 500 self-owned vehicles. Read more
