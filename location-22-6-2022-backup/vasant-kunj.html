Agarwal Packers and Movers Vasant Kunj

Vasant Kunj is a neighbourhood located in Delhi, India. This area is also home to several prominent personalities in India. Vasant Kunj had a population of 1,45,174 people according to the 2011 India census, with 65,253 female population and 79,921 male population. The size of the area is approximately 13.78 square kilometres.

Vasant Kunj is a moderately large area in Delhi. However, this place is a posh area with all requirements such as hospitals, schools, shopping centres, public transportation, etc.

Climate

The climate of Vasantkunj is an overlap between monsoon-influenced humid subtropical and semi-arid, with high variation between summer and winter temperatures and precipitation. The rainfall is deficient ( annually 250mm) in this region, and summer is very dry.

How to Reach

By Air
The nearest airport in the Vasantkunj region is Indira Gandhi International Airport. The distance between Indira Gandhi International airport and Vasantkunj station is only 4km. After you arrive at the airport, you can easily take a taxi or cab to the Vasant Kunj area. You can also take a bus from Mehrauli Terminal, but its time-consuming.

By Train
Both Delhi Railway Station and Anand Vihar Railway Station connect the city to all major Indian cities, including Delhi, Jaipur, Mumbai, Goa, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Kolkata, and Patna. Passengers can reach any of the above-mentioned railway stations and take buses from the nearest bus terminal as Vasantkunj is in the middle of these two stations. Passengers can also take taxis or cabs.

By Road
The best way to go is to take buses or the metro. As the metro transportation system is remarkable here. Buses always pass the Vasantkunj area so you can take a bus from several bus terminals to reach there at a low cost. The bus transportation system is remarkable here.

Closest Branch

The closest branch is situated 31 Km away from Vasant Kunj. You can take a bus from Masoodpur Dairy and then take a metro from Anand Vihar Metro Station. You can also book a cab and reach your destination.
