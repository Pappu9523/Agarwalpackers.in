International Packers and Movers: What to Keep in Mind and Other Tips for Moving Out

Moving to a new home, whether it's across the street or the world, can be a major undertaking. Packing up your entire life and transporting it to a new location can be stressful and overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. With proper planning and organisation, you can make your move a smooth and stress-free experience.

This article will share some essential tips and checklists for a successful and organised move. It will draw on advice from experts in packing and moving and insights from people who have experienced the joys and challenges of relocating their homes. Even though international packers and movers take the utmost care with your relocation, these tips will likely be valuable. Whether planning an international move or just moving down the block, our guide will help you keep everything in mind and ensure your move is successful.

Who are international packers and movers?

Packers and movers are businesses that assist in relocating households and other items safely to different locations inside the country or to different countries, ensuring complete security. They are experts in relocating households, corporations, and industrial goods from one country to another. They provide various services, including packing, shipping, and customs clearance. With their extensive experience and expertise, they can help you navigate the complex process of international relocation. They offer pre-move surveys to help you better plan your move and provide valuable tips and information to ensure your goods are packed and shipped according to international standards. Depending on your needs, they use air, sea, or road transportation and carefully select the shipping carrier to ensure the shortest transit durations. They also offer additional services like pest control, handyman services, home cleaning, and relocation services, including temporary housing, school searches, and city orientation. They use world-class packing materials, trained manpower, and advanced technology to move your goods safely and efficiently to your new country. Trust International Packers and Movers to make your international move stress-free and successful.

What are the benefits of international packers and movers?

Here are some reasons you would need an international packer and mover company for your international relocation.

Save your precious time and energy

Moving to a new country can be incredibly overwhelming, and there are a million things to care for all at once. That's where International Packers and Movers come in. With their help, you can save yourself a lot of time and energy. They take care of everything from packing your belongings to navigating customs, leaving you free to focus on other important tasks.

Safety of your packaging

When moving abroad on your own, you'll probably find that your hasty packing often compromises the safety of the packaging in some way. Hiring an international professional packer and mover service can prevent these tasks from becoming overwhelming.

Fixing your schedule

Hiring international packers and movers can save you time and hassle when planning and executing a successful move. Unlike relying on friends or family, who may cancel at the last minute, professional movers are reliable and efficient and ensure a smooth transition to your new home.

Customer service

A professional international packer and mover company would always be as helpful as possible to their customers. You can expect amazing customer service from such a trusted company.

Checklists and Tips for the International Movement

Here are some important tips and checklists for your upcoming international movement. Follow these tips for a better and hassle-free relocation.

Make a checklist of things you need to accomplish before relocating.

Before the relocation date starts knocking at the door, it is better to think of the tasks or activities you need to accomplish for a safe relocation. The checklist can go like this:

  • Things to be relocated
    • Kitchen utilities
    • Show-pieces
    • All types of clothes
    • Important official or professional documents
    • Laptop, TV, CDs, DVDs, and other electronic items

      • Now the large things like the almirah, furniture, bed, etc.

  • Which are the most important things among them?
  • Which items are most prone to damage or breaking?
  • Safely carrying the vehicles (two-wheelers and four-wheelers).
  • Organising everything properly.

Check out the best packing and moving service and book it

Until now, you have already understood that a packer and mover service is essential for international movement. So, do not hesitate to hire one. Check online for different international packer and mover services and the types of services they offer. It would be best if you hired a service that met all your requirements in one place.

Keep an eye on the calendar

Do not forget your date of relocation. Keeping an eye on the calendar can give you the sense of urgency to organise everything properly. It is better to plan everything 2-3 months before the exact date of relocation.

Declutter

It is not necessary to carry all the things in your household. When preparing to move, it's important to declutter your home by removing things you no longer use or need, such as old gadgets or items that don't match your new home's decor. It can be advantageous to consider parting ways with large items in your home, even if it means selling them, as it can help make the entire process of moving smoother and less stressful. Consider the cost of moving each item and decide if it's worth it. Create a separate declutter box for each room and begin the process at least two-three weeks before moving day. Donating unused clothes and items is also a good way to simplify the moving process.

Buy stuff like boxes and box-cutters, scissors, tape, etc.

Stocking up on necessary supplies a few days before the move is important to avoid the hassle of going to the store during the packing process. These supplies include boxes, box cutters, adhesive bandages, permanent markers, packing tape, paper towels, and garbage bags. Even if not used during the move, they will be useful afterwards. For larger moving equipment, renting tools from a moving company or purchasing them if you move frequently is recommended. It's crucial to have furniture dollies, pads or covers, and tie-down straps or rope readily available during the move, whether you buy, rent, or borrow them.

Put items from different rooms in separate boxes and label each box with the room it is destined for and its contents.

To simplify the unpacking process, keeping goods from various rooms separate while packing your belongings for a move is crucial. When it comes time to unpack, mixing belongings from various rooms in the same box might cause confusion and stress. Make sure you put goods from each room in distinct boxes and mark them appropriately to prevent this. It also makes it simpler for the movers to put the boxes in the right room at your new house, which aids in unpacking. Additionally, giving each box a brief description of what's inside will help you immediately identify particular things, like kitchenware or bedding, that you need. Your move and unpacking will go much more quickly and smoothly if you take the time to label your boxes properly.

Discuss things with your packer and mover service

Packers and moversare highly professional people with years of experience in international relocations. They would know much better about what to carry and what not to. So, do not hesitate to sit with them and take an hour or two to plan things out about the things you will need while relocating. They can also give you ideas about what you should not carry while moving to a specific country because different countries have different regulations.

Take the most important things with you

If it is not very hectic to carry, the most important things, such as laptops, essential documentation, etc., should be there with you. Important papers like ID cards, passports, and others must always be carried in your backpack. The night before the move, prepare a bag or suitcase with essential items such as clothes, toiletries, important documents, and toys for kids. Keep it with you during the move in case the moving truck gets lost or delayed.

Inquire with your mover service about specialized crates for transporting valuable artwork

A valuable artwork is something you do not want to spoil. Packers and movers may not be as careful as you are with the artwork. So ask them to take special care of those artworks. Avoid using regular paper to wrap oil paintings; they can stick to the surface. For pictures, place masking tape across the glass in an X shape to reinforce it and prevent it from breaking. Bubble wrap may work best for packing them, and putting them inside a frame box is better. To avoid damage during transport, it is advisable to insert a piece of cardboard between each framed piece for added protection.

Putting Things Together

Here is a short checklist for a quick look.

  • Start planning 2-3 months earlier.
  • Categorise the types of things you will move. List the things.
  • Buy boxes and put things in them. Make separate boxes for separate rooms and add labels about the things inside.
  • Declutter as much as possible. Sell, donate, or put the things you do not need to relocate in the trash.
  • Take the help of packers and movers to move your items.
  • Keep the most important things with you, especially important documents.
  • Be cautious with different pieces of artwork.

Conclusion

Even though moving abroad can seem daunting, it can be made much simpler with careful planning and preparation. This guide has given you practical advice on packing, organising, labelling, and transporting your belongings securely and successfully. You can ensure your move goes as smoothly as possible by following these instructions and working with international packers and movers. It will free up your time, so you can concentrate on settling into your new home and taking in your surroundings. Throughout the process, don't forget to take care of yourself, stay organised, and communicate clearly with your movers.
