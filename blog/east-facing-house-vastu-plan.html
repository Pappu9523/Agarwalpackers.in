Vastu Plan For East Facing House - Everything You Need to Know


When it comes to designing a house according to Vastu principles, the orientation of the house plays a vital role. An east-facing house is considered highly auspicious in Vastu Shastra, as it aligns with the rising sun and allows the flow of positive energy into the living space.Many individuals prefer an East Facing house Vastu plan in 3D for several reasons. An East Facing house is believed to attract positive energy, as it aligns with the direction of the rising sun. If you are seeking the best Vastu tips for an East Facing home, you have come to the right place.

In this post, we will unveil the best practices for designing your East Facing home in accordance with Vastu principles. It is essential to consider these guidelines to ensure a harmonious living space that promotes well-being and prosperity. By incorporating Vastu-friendly room placement, main entrance positioning, color choices, and interior design, you can create a balanced environment that resonates with positive vibrations. Additionally, implementing Vastu remedies, if necessary, can rectify any existing Vastu doshas and further enhance the energy flow in your home.

Before seeking Vastu plans for your East Facing home, you need a detailed understanding of the significance of Vastu Shastra. It is an ancient science rooted in India that deals primarily with the architecture of a home. The Vastu Shastra also emphasizes the significance of directions, energies coming from nature, and the elements.

The importance of Vastu Shastra is evident in the development of a pleasant and lively environment you can live in. Vastu Shastra perceives a combination of positive and negative energies everywhere and focuses on eliminating negative energies. Experts suggest that planning a home's design, according to Vastu, helps in fostering wealth, happiness, health, and prosperity for residents.

Are East Facing Houses Associated with Good Luck?

The east is where the sun rises and signifies health, vitality, hope, prosperity, and new beginnings. However, it is also important to note whether east facing house Vastu works well in all cases. All the East Facing homes don't guarantee all the positive outcomes you expect. Why? According to Vastu Shastra, the direction of your house is just one aspect to consider when assessing the potential luck and fortune it may bring.

Vastu Tips for East Facing House

Vastu Shastra suggests that East Facing properties attract auspicious outcomes, particularly in apartments and buildings. Here are some Vastu tips for an East Facing home.

  • Use a magnetic compass to measure the accurate east direction of your home.
  • Design your staircase in the west or south of your home.
  • An East Facing house Vastu plan with a pooja room would place the pooja room in the northeast direction. Vastu Shastra suggests that the northeast direction invites mental peace and brings good luck to the home.
  • The home's open spaces, such as balconies, should always be designed on the east face of the home.
  • You must place the living room in the northeast as it brings prosperity and fortune to your East Facing home.
  • The ideal positioning for guest rooms is in the northwest direction.
  • You have to design the master bedroom in the southwest direction.
  • The placement of the kitchen in a Vastu plan for your East Facing home presents two distinct options. You can design the kitchen in the southeast or the northwest direction. It is important to ensure that the person cooking food must face a specific direction in each alternative. You have to face east in a kitchen in the southeast direction and west in a kitchen in the northwest.
  • The walls in the north and east directions must be comparatively thinner and shorter than the south and west walls.
  • Place bowls of rock salt at different corners of the house to absorb all negative energies.
  • If you have a student in the home, then you must add a crystal globe in the northeast direction.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Main Gate



The main gate of an east-facing house is an important element for maintaining positive energy flow. Here are some Vastu tips for the main gate of an east-facing house:

  • Placement: The main gate should be positioned in the northeast or north direction of the house. This allows the morning sunlight to enter, bringing positive energy.
  • Size and Shape: The gate should be larger than the other doors in the house. A rectangular or square-shaped gate is considered auspicious.
  • Materials: Choose sturdy and high-quality materials for the gate, such as wood or metal. Avoid using materials like glass or plastic.
  • Opening and Closing: The main gate should open inwards, preferably clockwise. It should open smoothly without any obstructions.
  • Auspicious Symbols: Adorn the gate with auspicious symbols like Swastika or Om for attracting positive energy. Avoid any negative or cluttered elements.
  • Lighting: Install bright and well-lit lights near the gate to enhance the positive energy during the evenings.
  • Landscaping: Maintain a clean and clutter-free entrance with well-manicured plants and flowers. Avoid thorny plants near the gate.

Remember, the main gate is the entry point for energy into your home, so following these Vastu guidelines can help create a harmonious and welcoming environment.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Master Bedroom



The master bedroom is a sanctuary for rest and relaxation. Here are some Vastu tips to ensure positive energy in an east-facing master bedroom:

  • Placement: Ideally, the master bedroom should be located in the southwest corner of the house. If that's not possible, choose the southeast corner as the next best option.
  • Bed Placement: Position the bed in such a way that the headboard is on the south or west wall. Avoid placing the bed under a beam or directly in line with the door.
  • Colors: Opt for soothing and neutral colors for the bedroom walls, such as light shades of blue, green, or beige. These colors promote a calm and peaceful atmosphere.
  • Furniture Placement: Place heavy furniture, like wardrobes or almirahs, in the southwest corner of the room. Keep the center of the room open and clutter-free.
  • Mirrors: Avoid placing mirrors in front of the bed or facing the bed directly. If you must have a mirror, ensure that it is not reflecting the bed while you sleep.
  • Electronics: Keep electronic devices like televisions, computers, or exercise equipment away from the bedroom. If they are present, cover them with a cloth when not in use.
  • Ventilation and Lighting: Ensure proper ventilation and natural light in the bedroom. Use curtains or blinds that can be easily opened during the day to allow sunlight to enter.

By following these Vastu principles, you can create a tranquil and harmonious atmosphere in your east-facing master bedroom, promoting restful sleep and rejuvenation.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Living Room



The living room is the central space for socializing and relaxation. Here are some Vastu tips for an east-facing living room:

  • Location: Ideally, the living room should be in the northeast or north direction of the house. This allows for the flow of positive energy and abundance.
  • Furniture Placement: Position the furniture, such as sofas and chairs, in the southwest or west direction of the living room. This creates a cozy and inviting seating arrangement.
  • Centerpiece: Place a beautiful and auspicious centerpiece, like a decorative lamp or a vibrant painting, on the eastern wall. It becomes a focal point and enhances positive energy.
  • Colors: Choose light and soothing colors for the living room walls, such as beige, light yellow, or cream. Avoid dark or vibrant colors that can create a chaotic atmosphere.
  • Electronic Appliances: Keep electronic appliances like the television or stereo system in the southeast corner. Ensure they are placed in a way that they don't dominate the room.
  • Natural Light: Allow ample natural light to enter the living room during the day. Use curtains or blinds that can be easily opened to let sunlight in, creating a vibrant ambiance.
  • Indoor Plants: Place healthy and vibrant indoor plants in the east or northeast corner of the living room. They add a touch of nature and purify the air.

By incorporating these Vastu guidelines, you can create a harmonious and positive living room space that promotes relaxation, social interaction, and overall well-being.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Kitchen



The kitchen is considered the heart of the home, where nourishment and sustenance are prepared. Here are some Vastu tips for an east-facing kitchen:

  • Location: Ideally, the kitchen should be in the southeast corner of the house. If that's not feasible, the next best option is the northwest corner. Avoid placing the kitchen in the northeast corner.
  • Cooking Direction: Position the stove or cooktop in such a way that while cooking, you face east. This aligns the energy flow with the rising sun and promotes positivity.
  • Storage: Arrange the storage cabinets and shelves in the southern and western walls of the kitchen. Keep them well-organized and clutter-free for a smooth workflow.
  • Sink Placement: Place the sink in the northeast or north direction of the kitchen. Ensure it is not directly in line with the cooking stove.
  • Ventilation: Proper ventilation is crucial in the kitchen to maintain a clean and fresh environment. Install windows or an exhaust fan on the eastern wall to allow the entry of fresh air.
  • Colors: Choose bright and cheerful colors for the kitchen walls, such as shades of yellow or orange. These colors stimulate appetite and create a lively atmosphere.
  • Fire and Water Elements: Keep the fire element (stove, oven) and water element (sink, water purifier) separate in the kitchen. This helps balance the energies and avoids conflicts.
  • Storage Organization: Maintain a systematic arrangement of kitchen utensils and ingredients. Keep commonly used items easily accessible and store sharp objects safely.
  • Natural Elements: Incorporate natural elements like indoor plants or a small herb garden in the kitchen. They bring freshness, positivity, and improve air quality.
  • Cleanliness: Ensure regular cleaning and proper hygiene in the kitchen. Dispose of any expired food items or clutter to maintain a healthy and positive environment.

By following these Vastu principles, you can create a kitchen space that promotes efficient cooking, positive energy, and a nourishing atmosphere for you and your family.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Dining Room



The dining room is where family members come together to share meals and build stronger bonds. To ensure positive energy flow according to Vastu Shastra in an East Facing House, locate the dining room in the east or southeast area. Place the dining table in the center or towards the west or south direction of the room. It is preferable to have a large window in the east direction, allowing natural light during mealtimes. Choose warm and inviting colors for the dining room walls, such as shades of brown or orange, fostering a pleasant and nourishing dining experience. By incorporating East Facing House Vastu principles, you can create a harmonious and auspicious environment for family meals.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Pooja Room



The pooja room holds immense spiritual significance, and its proper placement in an east-facing house as per Vastu Shastra is crucial for positive energy flow. Ideally, locate the pooja room in the northeast zone, following the principles of East Facing House Vastu. Ensure that it is clutter-free and well-ventilated, aligning with Vastu Shastra guidelines. Position the deity or idols facing east or west, allowing devotees to face east while offering prayers, as recommended by East Facing House Vastu. Use calming and sacred colors like white or light yellow for the walls, as per both Vastu Shastra and East Facing House Vastu. Soft lighting and the aroma of incense can create a serene ambiance, enhancing the spiritual atmosphere of the room in line with both Vastu Shastra and East Facing House Vastu principles.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Artefacts



The strategic placement of artifacts in an east-facing house can amplify positive energy and aesthetic appeal. Place artistic and decorative pieces in the north or east direction of the living room or common areas. Display paintings or artwork depicting natural landscapes or auspicious symbols like peacocks or lotus flowers. Avoid placing heavy or aggressive-looking sculptures. Ensure that the artifacts are well-lit, either by natural or artificial lighting, highlighting their beauty and attracting positive energy into the space.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Water Tank Placement



Proper placement of the water tank in an east-facing house ensures a harmonious water element. Ideally, position the water tank in the northeast zone, ensuring it is not in direct contact with the main building. The tank should be elevated and situated at a lower level than the house, allowing water to flow towards the northeast direction. Ensure that the tank remains clean and free from leaks. Placing a few plants near the tank can further enhance the positive energy associated with the water element.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Open Spaces or Balcony



Open spaces and balconies in an east-facing house offer opportunities to connect with nature and rejuvenate. Locate balconies or open spaces towards the east or northeast direction. This allows ample natural light and fresh air to flow into the house. Create a seating area or place potted plants in these spaces to establish a serene environment. Use light and soothing colors for the flooring or outdoor furniture. Incorporate greenery and flowering plants to promote positive energy and a sense of tranquility.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Ideal Wall Colours



Choosing the right wall colors can significantly impact the ambiance and energy flow in an east-facing house. Opt for light and soothing colors that reflect natural light, such as shades of white, cream, light yellow, or pastels. These colors create an airy and spacious feel, enhancing positive energy throughout the house. Avoid using dark or gloomy colors that absorb light and create a heavy atmosphere. Experiment with different color combinations while ensuring a harmonious blend that complements the overall aesthetics and promotes a positive living environment.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Staircase



The placement and design of the staircase in an east-facing house can influence the overall energy flow and well-being of its occupants. Ideally, position the staircase in the southwest or south-southwest direction. Ensure that the staircase ascends from east to west or south to north. Avoid spiral or circular staircases, as they can disrupt the flow of energy. Maintain proper lighting and ventilation around the staircase area. Incorporate safety features like handrails and non-slip materials to ensure a secure and harmonious transition between floors.

East Facing House Plan as per Vastu for Toilet



Although toilets are functional spaces, their Vastu-compliant design can contribute to the overall positive energy within an east-facing house. Locate toilets in the northwest or southeast direction. Ensure that they do not share a common wall with the pooja room or kitchen. Keep the toilet area clean, well-ventilated, and adequately lit. Use light colors for the walls and consider incorporating natural elements like indoor plants to neutralize any negative energy. Install efficient plumbing systems to prevent leaks or water-related issues, maintaining a hygienic and harmonious environment.

Things to Avoid in East Facing House as per Vastu Shastra

According to Vastu, East Facing homes have an aura of positivity around them with the proper adjustments. However, Vastu Shastra also points out some important things you must avoid when designing East Facing homes. Here is an outline of Vastu's tips for avoiding specific directions in the placement of different rooms and other elements.

  • A good 30x40 east facing house vastu plan would never place the toilets in the northeast corner of the home.
  • In addition, Vastu also recommends avoiding the construction of septic tanks in an East Facing home.
  • Never choose the northeast direction for your kitchen in an East Facing home.
  • The ideal Vastu plan for East Facing homes would advise against placing a staircase in the northeast corner.
  • Vastu Shastra also implies that you cannot have a north-to-south slope on the plot.
  • Do not place mirrors in front of the bed so that you don't see your reflection while in bed.
  • You must avoid placing the garage in the northeast for an East Facing home.
  • Your East Facing property must not have any large trees planted in the east or north direction.
  • The northeast corner of an East Facing home should not be blocked or heavily packed.
  • The home's main entrance should not have obstacles such as poles, statues, or trees.

Dos and Don'ts for Every Room in an East Facing House Vastu Plan

Planning your home's design, according to Vastu Shastra, comes with a huge responsibility. You must clearly understand the Vastu for different rooms in your East Facing home. Let us find out what you can and should not do for the Vastu-based design of different rooms.

Main Door

The placement of rooms is not the only priority in Vastu Shastra. You must also pay attention to the placement of the main entrance to your home. Avoid the southeast or northeast direction for the main door to your home.

It is important to ensure that the main entrance is far from the corners in the middle. You must ensure that the main entrance does not touch the northeast corner. The distance between the wall and the main door should be at least half feet. You can also consider some pointers for removing Vastu Dosh when you have the main entrance in the southeast direction.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom is one of the most significant aspects of a home. Vastu suggests that the southwest direction is the best choice for the placement of your master bedroom. In addition, you should also pay attention to making the bedroom bigger than the other rooms.

The ideal directions for placing the bed are the south or west walls. Make sure that the head of the bed is in the south or west direction. On top of it, you must ensure that the bathroom in this room never faces the bed. Its door should stay closed at all times. You must also avoid placing a mirror in front of the bed.

Pooja Room

The best pooja room east facing house vastu plan suggests the north-east direction as the best spot. In addition, you must ensure that the ceiling of the pooja room is lower compared to other rooms. Avoid the placement of a bathroom in front of the pooja room for your East Facing home.

Dining Room

The dining room in your East Facing home should be designed in the east direction. You should ensure that the dining room door never faces the main door. The seating arrangement in the dining room should avoid the south direction. Ideally, the head of the family should take a seat at the east end of the dining table.

Kitchen

Without proper alignment with Vastu Shastra guidelines, your kitchen might result in bad luck and misfortune.

The best-recommended positioning for the kitchen in your East Facing home is in the southeast direction. You can also go in the northwest direction as a reliable position for your kitchen. Avoid the north, west, and northeast direction while planning the kitchen's placement in your home.

Positioning of kitchen appliances is also an important highlight for Vastu-based design. Place refrigerators in the southwest direction. On the other hand, the cooking stove, microwave/oven, and toasters should stay in the southeast corner.

Water Tank

The water tank is an obvious necessity for your home. If you want an underground water tank, then north or northeast are the best positions for the tank. On the other hand, the west or southwest is the ideal direction for placing overhead water tanks. Most importantly, you should avoid placing a water tank at the center.

Toilet

The positioning of toilets is also an important concern in Vastu plans for an East Facing home. The right direction for a toilet in your home is important as it removes unwanted negativity and waste from your home. You should avoid designing it at the northeast or southeast corners as it can bring negative consequences. The ideal directions for placing your toilet include west of northwest, south of southwest, or east of the southeast corners.

Vastu Tips for East Facing Home FAQs

Is an East Facing house really lucky?

The East Facing house is auspicious in the case of buildings and apartments. However, according to Vastu Shastra, East Facing independent villas and bungalows can face some concerns.

What is the ideal position for the entrance door in an East Facing home?

The east direction is the ideal direction for an entrance door in East Facing homes. You must avoid the southeast and northeast corners for the entrance door placement.

Why is the orientation of an east-facing house significant in Vastu Shastra?

In Vastu Shastra, the orientation of a house plays a crucial role in harnessing positive energy. An east-facing house allows for the entry of morning sunlight, symbolizing vitality, health, and new beginnings. It is believed to attract positive vibrations and promote overall well-being.

What are the key considerations for the entrance and main door in an east-facing house?

The main entrance and door placement are vital in Vastu for an east-facing house. It is recommended to position the main entrance towards the northeast or east quadrant for optimal energy flow. The main door should be well-maintained, sturdy, and aesthetically pleasing. Avoid any obstructions or clutter near the entrance.

How should the rooms be arranged in an east-facing house Vastu plan?

Proper room arrangement is essential for maintaining Vastu harmony in an east-facing house. Place the master bedroom in the southwest corner for stability and prosperity. The kitchen should ideally be in the southeast corner, and the living room in the northeast or east quadrant. Ensure each room is well-lit and well-ventilated.

Are there any specific Vastu tips for interior design and decor in an east-facing house?

Yes, for interior design, choose light and soothing colors that promote positivity and create a sense of spaciousness. Arrange furniture in a manner that allows for free movement and promotes interaction. Incorporate Vastu-friendly decor elements such as indoor plants, sacred symbols, and artwork depicting nature.

How can one address Vastu doshas or imbalances in an east-facing house?

Vastu doshas can be remedied through various measures. For instance, if there is a Vastu dosha in the east direction, one can place mirrors or reflective surfaces strategically to enhance the energy flow. Additionally, performing regular Vastu rituals, such as cleansing with saltwater or burning incense, can help restore balance.

Can landscaping and outdoor elements impact the Vastu of an east-facing house?

Yes, landscaping and outdoor elements are crucial in Vastu. Maintain a well-groomed garden in the northeast quadrant of the property. Avoid placing heavy structures or trees in the east direction as they can obstruct the flow of positive energy. Incorporate water features like a fountain or pond in the northeast or east for auspiciousness.

Is it necessary to consult a Vastu expert for designing an east-facing house Vastu plan?

While it is not mandatory, consulting a Vastu expert can provide valuable insights and personalized recommendations based on your specific requirements. They can assess the layout, offer detailed guidance, and suggest remedies to mitigate any Vastu imbalances, ensuring the best possible Vastu plan for your east-facing house.
