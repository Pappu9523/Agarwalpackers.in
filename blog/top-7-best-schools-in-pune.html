Top 11 Best Schools in Pune


Good education is the key to a good livelihood. We all want our kids to have the best education possible and make it to the top of their fields. This education starts at the primary level in schools. Thus, picking the Right & Best school in Pune for your children can seem daunting. What should one look for in the Best schools of Pune? What criteria decide upon the quality of the school, the teachers, staff, extracurricular activities, and more in Pune?

We have created a list of the 7 Top & Best Schools in Pune to make your life easier. These schools provide the best facilities to nurture your children into excellence. We have listed the features of each of these schools and ensured that you get ample information about them.

List of Best Schools in Pune

The top 11 Schools in Pune are:

  • Indus International School Pune
  • Delhi Public School
  • Mahindra International School
  • MIT Pune Vishwashanti Gurukul
  • Sahyadri School
  • The Bishop School, Pune
  • Symbiosis International School, Viman Nagar, Pune
  • Pawar Public School, Hinjewadi, Pune
  • The Kalyani School
  • Army Public School
  • The Lexicon International School

Indus International School Pune

The Indus International School started its journey in 2008. In its first year, it had a mere 52 students. Today, it is one of the most popular schools in Pune. One of the reasons for this is the school's belief in holistic education for children. Not only does the Indus International School focus on the academic curriculum, but it also gives special attention to extracurriculars. Music, art, drama, dance, and sports are given due importance at this place, making it one of the best schools in Pune.

It has a green campus with state-of-the-art facilities for the students to provide them with a healthy, happy learning environment. There is also a leadership curriculum in the school that ensures every kid leaves here as a future leader who can lead society to greatness. Its emphasis on leadership makes it one of the best schools in Pune.

Keeping the parents in the loop is a good measure by Indus International School, Pune. With their Parent Advisory Committee, they ensure that they keep an open-door communication policy with the parents of their students. Apart from this, the school also provides weekend and full-time boarding for the parents.

Address: 576, Bhukum, Tal, Mulshi Rd, Pune, Maharashtra 412115

Delhi Public School

This school is nestled in Nyati County, Pune and is a chain of DPS schools. Delhi Public School follows values like being environmentally friendly and also promotes the conservation of natural resources. To exemplify, DPS schools utilised solar lights despite electricity to save energy.

The school is affiliated with the CBSE board, New Delhi, and offers schooling from class Nursery to Class XII. The school provides quality education, with most of their students passing their boards with a percentage of 90 or above.

Address: Nyati Estate Rd, Nyati County, Mohammed Wadi, Pune, Maharashtra 411060

Mahindra International School

Located in Hinjewadi, Pune, Mahindra International School is a favourite school not only among Indians or Punekars but also liked by students from several other countries. As a result, your child takes classes with a culturally diverse group.

Moreover, it is a co-ed school with classes from Nursery to XII. It is an English medium school affiliated with the International Baccalaureate (IB). Mahindra International School is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC) and the Council of International Schools (CIS) as well.

Address: P-26, MIDC, Hinjewadi Phase 1 Rd, Phase 1, Hinjewadi Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, Hinjawadi, Pune, Maharashtra 411057

MIT Pune Vishwashanti Gurukul

Nestled in Loni Kalbhor, Pune, MIT, Pune Vishwashanti Gurukul is known as the no. 1 boarding school in the city, as its name implies. They embrace both traditional and global teaching methods. They follow a "guru-shishya" relationship where students and teachers are encouraged to socialise outside of the classrooms. Moreover, their classes are entirely research-based and supported by the newest technologies in order to ensure that learning is not solely restricted to homework and tests.

Furthermore, the MIT Pune Vishwashanti Gurukul school is a co-ed institute affiliated with International Baccalaureate (IB) and Cambridge International Examinations (CIE). The school has clubs, organisations, and societies that plan numerous events to help each child grow academically and personally. These academic and co-curricular clubs are a fantastic way for students to discover their interests or maybe establish new ones.

Address: Loni Kalbhor, Maharashtra 412201

Sahyadri School

Sahyadri School in Pune is among the best schools, ranked among the top 10 co-ed boarding schools in India. It is a residential school that has an ICSE curriculum. The school was established in 1995 and managed by the Krishnamurti Foundation India.

The school offers admissions to students from classes 4th-10th. Focusing on overall development, it emphasises robust curricular and extracurricular activities. It has all the facilities required to aid the child in learning, from modern computer labs and science laboratories to a good library and sports grounds. The school also provides transport for the students. All these facilities make it one of the best schools in Pune.

The school follows the principles of the philosophical Speaker, J. Krishnamurti. It also emphasises quality leadership in students, organising activities, and creating an environment that can nurture leadership abilities in the students. With an overall emphasis on teaching what is best for students, Sahyadri School is indeed one of the best schools in Pune for your child.

Address: Rajgurunagar, Dist. Pune, Tiwai Hill, Maharashtra 410513

The Bishop School, Pune

The third school on our list is The Bishop School. It is a boarding cum day school for boys. It is open for students seeking admission from LKG to class 12th. The school follows the curriculum of the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE).

The school is well known for its experienced and knowledgeable faculty and unique teaching methodologies that foster the needs of the students. The advanced infrastructure of the school makes it one of the best schools in Pune.

It is also one of the oldest schools in Pune, which started as early as 1864, with around 15 boys as students. Today, this strength has increased significantly. While it has ensured that it keeps pace with all the modern educational developments, it has not forgotten its core values. Some of the school's architecture also remains intact from its olden day, giving it a classy look. Many students say that the buildings have a canny similarity to Hogwarts, a fictional school in the Harry Potter series.

Address: 5-A, General Bhagat Marg, Camp, Pune, Maharashtra 411001

Symbiosis International School, Viman Nagar, Pune

Symbiosis College in Pune is one of the most popular colleges in India. It also has a school in its name in Pune. The Symbiosis International School is one of the best schools in Pune. The school is located at Viman Nagar, near an airport. It provides ample opportunities for the students to grow into the best version of themselves.

TThe school was established in 2005. It is a co-ed school affiliated with the International Baccalaureate Organisation and the Cambridge International Examinations. Being affiliated with these top-notch organisations gives the Symbiosis International School an edge over the other schools in Pune. Their curriculum and teaching staff are one of the best that you will find. They have various unique programs like the IB Primary Years Programme and the IB Diploma program for its students. Maintaining a healthy student-to-teacher ratio of 12:1 also holds the school in good stead with respect to education. Sending your child here will be wise and help them achieve their dreams.

Address: Gate No. 3A, Symbiosis old campus, off Symbiosis Road Formerly, off New Airport Road, Viman Nagar, Pune, Maharashtra 411014

Pawar Public School, Hinjewadi, Pune

Founded in 2010, the Pawar Public School is also one of the best schools in Pune. The Pawar Public Charitable Trust manages this co-ed school. It is affiliated with the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) and is open for students from Nursery to class 10th.

The school is recognised for having a good science lab and organising plenty of guest lectures by foreign lecturers. Their student exchange program is also excellent, as it exposes your kids to international students and allows them to go abroad. They organise regular workshops so the students can gain practical experience. Apart from this, they also have a separate Robotics program for high school students, giving them a taste of engineering at this young age.

Having one of the highest average scores in ICSE exams (90%) speaks volumes about the academic abilities of the school. These qualities make this school one of the most efficient and quality schools in Pune.

Address: Megapolis, Siraj Dokadia Rd, Sahyadri Park, Plot No. 2 & 3, Phase 3, Hinjewadi Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, Hinjawadi, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra 411057

The Kalyani School

The Kalyani school was officially established in 2015 as a Co-ed school. It follows the curriculum of the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The school has garnered a good reputation because of its dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art campus. All these features make it one of the best schools in Pune.

The school is managed by a trust called Akutai Kalyani Charitable. It is an English medium school that provides admission to students from LKG to class 8th. The school is spread across 9.5 acres, providing a modern environment for all its students. The school prides itself on providing a safe and healthy environment for students to think out of the box and be creative. Having a school that taps into the child's creative side is something that we all desire, which makes Kalyani school one of the best schools in Pune.

Address: Near Hadapsar, Manjari Budruk, Pune, Maharashtra 412307

Army Public School

Army Public School was established in the year 1988, and in today's times ranks amongst the best schools in not only Pune but the entire country. The school provides admission to students from classes 1 to 12th. It is an English medium school and is often credited with being one of the best schools in Pune.

The school has plenty of academic reputation, thanks to students who have scored well consistently. Apart from this, there is also a lot of focus on life skills, sports education, conflict management, etc., for the holistic development of the students. An environment that focuses on building leadership qualities and provides ample support to the students to delve into their creative side makes this school one of the best in Pune. It has a healthy teacher-student ratio and has a dedicated staff to ensure that the students don't lack anything.

Facilities like Music rooms and Student meditation corners provided by this school show that they also cater to students' mental health. A school that cares deeply for a student and does not just focus on the formal syllabi is a school that will shape the future of your child. That is what makes this school one of the best schools in Pune.

Address: Ghorpadi Rd, Opp Bank of Maharashtra, Ghorpuri Lines, Dobarwadi, Ghorpadi, Pune, Maharashtra 411001

The Lexicon International School

Army Public School was established in the year 1988, and in today's times ranks amongst the best schools in not only Pune but the entire country. The school provides admission to students from classes 1 to 12th. It is an English medium school and is often credited with being one of the best schools in Pune.

Moreover, the school recently hosted a session for 10th-grade students with their seniors of 12th grade. The main aim of this session was to decrease the stress associated with selecting and deciding the field.

The Lexicon International School is a Senior Secondary School affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). It is a co-ed school with classes from Nursery to XII. The school works in two batches, i.e. Dewdrops and Sunshine. The timings for Dewdrops are 8:20 am to 11:00 am, and for sunshine batches, the timings are 11:10 am to 1:50 pm. The concept of two batches allows children to focus on their co-curricular activities smoothly along with their studies.

Address: Street 1, Phase 1, near MRF Tyre Showroom, Rao Tularam Vihar, Rewari, Haryana 123401


Conclusion

The destiny of a country is shaped in its classrooms. The children are the future of the country, and it is on them that we depend on them to take our country to new heights. And to do this, we need them to have the best education possible. In today's time, education is not just about facts but a student's overall personality development to prepare them for the world.

Education today has become more of a business; hence we find a lot of schools. We must recognise the right school for our children, the ones that are modern and have the right environment and facilities to prepare the child for the world outside.
