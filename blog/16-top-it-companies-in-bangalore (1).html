Top 16 IT Companies in Bangalore

Bangalore is known to be the leading information technology hub of the nation having Best IT Companies in Bangalore, which is why it's called the "Silicon Valley of India." Due to this reason, many talents come to try their career here. Bangalore IT companies are popular for giving opportunities to freshers and also building career steps for experienced employees. From well-established Bangalore IT company to unique startup, Bangalore city has all that every tech employee is looking for.

1. Infosys

Infosys Ltd. is one of the top Indian multinational corporations in the IT industry that is well-known across the globe. It is one of the best Bangalore IT companies that deals with IT, business consultation, and various software outsourcing services such as data analytics, IoT, AI, etc. Founded by Narayana Murthy, Infosys first operated in Pune in 1981 before shifting to Bangalore in 1983. Now the current managing director of Infosys is Salil Parekh. The corporation offers evolutionary ways various domain companies operate with various business solutions.

2. Wipro

Wipro is another IT giant in the city that deals with IT software, AI, data analytics, IoT, and more. Wipro offers business solutions by assisting in customer behavior prediction, which ultimately helps companies to provide enhanced customer experience.

Compared to other top IT companies in Bangalore, Wipro has made great advancements in the business application area. Although established in Amalner, Maharashtra, in 1945 by Mohamed Premji, Bangalore now houses the head office of Wipro in India.

3. Accenture

Accenture is among the many IT companies in Bangalore, which has become a big workplace offering IT software, data analytics, IoT, and AI. This company is now one of the largest IT consulting companies with hands-on strategy and digital solutions.

Accenture is an American Irish multinational company whose head office is in Bengaluru. Due to driving innovation and unmatched technical strategy, Accenture is now part of the Fortune Global 500, improving how the world and lives operate in a digitalized sphere.

4. Oracle

Oracle is another big-shot corporation with ten offices in Bangalore. Oracle deals with providing a comprehensive and fully integrated tower of cloud applications and service platforms. Its headquarter is in Redwood Shores, California, functioning under the guidance of Larry Ellison. Oracle holds the title of being the third largest software company in Bangalore by revenue.

5. TCS

Tata Consultancy Services Limited, or TCS, is a big Indian multinational IT services and consulting company operating as a subsidiary of the Tata Group in 149 locations across 46 countries. Apart from Infosys, TCS is also one of the oldest IT companies in India and is among the top MNC IT companies in Bangalore, with its headquarters in Mumbai. Its expertise in offering consultancy services to various industries worldwide attracts a high talent pool.

6. Google

Established as one of the IT giant companies, Google is almost every technocrat's dream. If someone gets to start their career at Google, it would be a great landmark in their resume. If it's not already known, Google is an American multinational company with expertise in everything associated with internet services, including search engines, cloud computing, online advertising technologies, software, and hardware. Known as one of the big four IT companies alongside Apple, Facebook, Amazon, and Google, it has a branch in Bangalore.

7. Microsoft

Microsoft is one of the leading software systems and applications developers for personal computers, mobile devices, and many other smart devices. This is one of the top Bangalore IT companies, an American multinational technology corporation headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Its segments include business processes, productivity, Intelligent Cloud, etc. The corporation also deals with publishing books and multimedia titles, developing its line of hybrid tablet computers, email service, electronic game systems, and computer input and output devices.

8. Intel

Intel is known as the largest computer chip-making corporation in the world. It is an American multinational corporation with its headquarters in Santa Clara, California. Intel has been in India for a decade and has five offices in Bangalore. Apart from developing the microprocessors like semiconductor computer circuits, Intel is also expertise in manufacturing network interface controllers, motherboard chipsets, flash memory, and diverse devices associated with computing and communications.

9. IBM

International Business Machines Corporation, also known as IBM, is a leading expert in coding, design, IT consulting, etc. This is one of the leading cloud structure IT companies in Bangalore that assists IoT, frameworks services, research, experience configuration, and cloud services. Operating for over 100 years, the company has been running its corporation in more than 170 countries, including India. Most companies outsource IBM teams to work on research and development projects in Bangalore.

10. Cognizant

Founded in 1994, Cognizant, an American MNC, has advanced greatly by providing tech solutions, business counseling, framework combination, application, IT, and research. Cognizant helps businesses shift digitally in a more seamless way with modern technology, change customers' experience, and re-structure operational procedures to adapt to the fast-changing competitive business world. This company is one of the best IT companies in Bangalore that offers ample opportunities for freshers to grow in their careers.

11. Cisco

Cisco Systems Inc. is another American multinational technology corporation listed among the top Bangalore IT companies. This company has expertise in forming an easier way to link various types of computer systems. Cisco develops, manufactures, and sells software, networking hardware, telecommunication equipment, high-end technology services, products, etc. This legendary company pioneered the concept of LAN (Local Area Network) for connecting different types of computer systems over the multiprotocol router system.

12. Capgemini

Capgemini SE is a French Multinational corporation that offers a range of integrated services incorporating critical technology. The company provides consultation, technological solutions, and professional outsourcing services. Till now, Capgemini has established over ten offices in India, and the IT city Bangalore is one of them. Many businesses approach and rely on Capgemini for its designing services, advanced digital and technological transformation, business management administrations, AI, custom solution improvement, application management, etc. digital services.

13. Mindtree

Mindtree Ltd. is a leading Indian MNC that is a part of the Larsen & Toubro Group. The company offers technology consulting, data analysis, and transformation by integrating AI to produce and deliver data-driven customer experiences. Established in 1999, the company has been taking on cloud computing and data analytics projects for various computer-aided software and mobile applications. The company aids its clients to better engage in the digital business environment by simplifying digital complexity with new IT initiatives, so businesses can market faster by employing emerging technologies and their effectiveness.

14. HCL

HCL Technologies Ltd. is an Indian Multinational IT services and consulting company, a subsidiary branch of HCL Enterprise that ranks among the top 5 IT companies in India. Founded by Shiv Nadar, the organization has its headquarters in Noida, but five of its offices are established in Bangalore.

The corporation enables various business enterprises to re-imagine and change their business strategies by implementing Digital technology transformation. Its expertise offers various infrastructure and software services for business process outsourcing. HCL mostly works with industries like finance, telecommunication, manufacturing, publication, media, healthcare, life science, etc.

15. Amazon

Amazon is another name among the well-known IT brands, which has also established one of its branches in the IT city, Bangalore. This American MNC also focuses on e-Commerce, digital streaming, cloud computing, and IT. Established in 1994, Amazon has become a cloud computing giant worldwide.

Amazon has an inclusive work culture and massive opportunities with advanced tools and technologies to enhance worker working experience, gain various perspectives, and become resourceful. Amazon is designing unique products and service ideas to adapt to new challenges in its everyday operation.

16. HP

The Hewlett-Packard Company, shortened to HP, is an American multinational IT company specializing in developing personal computers, printers, associative supplies, and advanced 3D printing solutions. Its hardware and software-related services are required in small and medium-sized business corporations and many individual customers. Apart from that, its consumers are also in the health, educational, and government sectors. Since India is one of the brand's big retail outlets, HP has many offices in Bangalore, with its headquarters in Palo Alto, California.

These are some of the top IT software companies in Bangalore, the IT capital of India. Apart from these top companies, other well-known IT companies in Bangalore are Mu Sigma, Microland, Flipkart, and TESCO Hindustan.
