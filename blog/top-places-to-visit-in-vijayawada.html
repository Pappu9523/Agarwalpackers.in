Top 10 Places to Visit in Vijayawada in 2023

Vijaywada is the city of temples and festivals, it is one of the significant commercial hubs and is the third largest city in Andhra Pradesh after Visakhapatnam and Tirupati by which there are many places to visit in Vijayawada in 2023 mentioned below in this Blog. Also, the city was once known as Beedah Veedah because of the large pandanus tree that existed here.

All in all, Vijayawada will surely take your heart away with its liveliness and its Top Most Places to visit in Vijayawada. If you have always dreamt of traveling to the land of pandanus trees, you have come to the right place.

List of Places to Visit in Vijayawada

We have prepared a list of the best places from which you can make a list of top 10 places to visit in Vijayawada that will make your journey more beautiful and fascinating.

  • Undavalli Caves, Penumaka
  • Moghalrajpuram Caves
  • Bhavani Island
  • Victoria Jubilee Museum
  • Kondapalli Fort
  • Gandhi Hill
  • Kanaka Durga Temple
  • Hazratbal Mosque
  • Rajiv Gandhi Park
  • Akkanna Madanna Caves
  • Gunadala Matha Shrine
  • VMC Disney Land
  • Nallamala Hills
  • Araku Valley
  • Lambasingi Hills

Undavalli Caves, Penumaka

The rock-cut temples at the Undavalli Caves date back 1,300 years. A massive collection of 64 caves constructed between the second and seventh centuries, the Undavalli Caves are devoted to the Hindu and Buddhist religions.

A monolithic statue of Lord Vishnu in a reclined position made of black granite is housed in this cave. Numerous more caves are devoted to various Hindu gods, including Lord Shiva, Lord Brahma, and their avatars. They reflect the Gupta dynasty’s architectural style. The caves are undoubtedly worth a visit because they are embellished with various magnificent sculptures.

Address: FHWHJ+VV4, Penumaka - Vijayawada Road Near Prakasam, Barrage, Undavalli, Andhra Pradesh 522501

Timings: 9am–5:30pm

Moghalrajpuram Caves

The Moghalrajpuram caves have three temples and are one of the national monuments of primary importance. Mogalaraja caves have the idols of Lord Nataraja and Ganesh that are still in fair shape. The Ardhanarishvara statue in this Mogalarajapuram temple is the oldest in South India.

Address: GJ4W+FPR, Siddhartha College Rd, Moghalrajpuram, Christurajupuram, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520010

Timings: 9am-7pm

Bhavani Island

In calm waters lies Bhavani Island, one of the most popular tourist destinations in Vijayawada, being enjoyed equally by locals and visitors. Surrounded by mangroves, the island is ideal for enjoying evening walks and peaceful fresh air.

With the excellent amenities the Andhra Government provides, you can relish swimming, and boat rides here. One can participate in water sports and adventurous activities on this lovely island.

Address: River Krishna, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, 521225, India

Timings: 12am – 11:59 pm

Victoria Jubilee Museum

Victoria Museum is well known for preserving an extensive collection of ancient sculptures, idols, paintings, inscriptions, weaponry, and silverware.

Additionally, the Victoria Museum houses neolithic and microlithic tools, weapons, gold and silver coins, manuscripts, pottery, and prehistoric stone tools. The Buddha statues made of granite and white limestone are the center attractions of the Museum.

Address: Bapu Museum, MG Rd, Buckinghampeta, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520002

Timings: 10:30am–5pm

Kondapalli Fort

Kondapalli Fort was built as a three-storey-tall rock tower. The fort has three entrances, with the main gateway, known as Dargah Darwazah, constructed from a single granite block. At the bottom of the hill, on the other side of the Golconda Darwazah entrance gate, is the settlement of Jaggaiahpet village.

The main draw of Kondapalli fort, Tanish Mahal or Palace, is situated on a summit between two hills. The palace is close to a deep reservoir with extremely cold water. The toys made of light wood that can be found on the hill in the Kondapalli village are renowned worldwide.

Address: GGJ+57C, Ghat Road, Kondapalli, Andhra Pradesh 521228

Timings: 8am-5pm

Gandhi Hill

Gandhi Hill is the first Gandhi memorial in the nation located on a hillock. It is a popular tourist destination near Vijayawada. It features seven stupas and was built to commemorate the nation's founding fathers. The main attraction is the 52-foot-tall Gandhi stupa. The Light and Sound show sheds light on the journey of Gandhi's life and his core teachings. You will find the Gandhi Memorial Library a safe space if you love books.

Address: Jawaharlal Nehru Road, K. R. Market, Tarapet, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, 520001, India

Timings: 4:30 pm – 8:30 pm

Kanaka Durga Temple

The well-known Kanaka Durga temple is a main attraction of Vijayawada. This temple is nestled on the Indrakeeladri hill by the banks of the River Krishnapopular folklore says that the temple was built by one of the great warriors of Mahabharta named Arjuna.

Address: Indrakeeladri, Mallikarjunapeta, Durga Temple Ghat Road, Durga Agraharam, Durga Agraharam, Mallikarjunapeta, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, 520001, India

Timings: 4:00 am – 5:45 pm (Dharma Darshan & Mukha Mandapam)
5:00 am – 5:45 pm (Antaralayam Darshan)
6:15 pm – 10:00 pm (Dharma Darshan, Mukha Mandapam & Antaralayam Darshan)

Hazratbal Mosque

Among Vijayawada's Muslim worshippers, the Hazratbal Mosque occupies a prominent position. The mosque is highly known for its exquisite architecture. A holy artifact belonging to the Prophet Mohammed makes it a major attraction for Muslim devotees. As a result, worshippers from all across the nation come to this mosque to pray.

Address: Vijayawada 520001, India

Timings: 09:30 am to 05:30

Rajiv Gandhi Park

Rajiv Gandhi Park attracts tourists with its great network of horticulture. This park is incredibly appealing due to the broad range of floral species planted on the rich soil from the banks of the River Krishna.

Other attractions include an aquarium and miniature zoo, play area, skating rink, rock fall, miniature railroad, gun shooting range, and jeep ride. Both children and adults can enjoy the musical fountain, which is open in the evenings. There are summertime activities for the locals to enjoy.

Address: NH65, Krishnalanka, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, 520001, India

Timings: 2:00 pm – 9:00 pm

Akkanna Madanna Caves

In Vijayawada, Akkanna Madonna caves are among the most well-known and historical places to explore. These 17th-century caves, essentially temples, contain numerous idols, including those of Vishnu, Brahma, and Shiva.

Address: GJ74+CJM, Arjuna St, Durga Agraharam, Mallikarjunapeta, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520001

Timings: 24 Hours

Gunadala Matha Shrine

The Gunadala Matha Shrine, also known as St. Mary's Church, is concealed in the hills of Krishna District and offers hope and comfort to many. During the joyous celebration of Our Lady of the Lourdes’, the crowd is lively and full of celebratory vibes.

Address: GMC5+FQ8, Eluru Rd, Lurdhunagar, Christurajapuram, Gunadala, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520004

Timings: 6:30am–7:30pm

VMC Disney Land

One of the most well-known and biggest water parks in Vijayawada, is VMC Disney Land. It is located in Ajit Singh Nagar and is considered a favorite hangout for localities because it offers separate swimming pools for professionals, adults, beginners, and children. It is full of water sports, fun games, thrilling rides, aqua dancing, wave pools, etc.

Address: Pipula Road, Junction, Ajit Singh Nagar, Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh 520015

Timings: 11am-5pm

Nallamala Hills

One of Andhra Pradesh's most well-known hill resorts is Nallamala Hills. These Hills are home to several significant temples in addition to being a component of one of India's largest tiger sanctuaries. The Nallamala Hills' originated due to historic volcanic activity and made unusual rock formations. Numerous areas in the state are covered with hills and are also well known for hiking and trekking.

Address: Rayalaseema, Andhra Pradesh.

Timings: 24 Hours

Araku Valley

The closest river rafting location to Vijayawada is Araku Valley. It is a town that is mainly a hill station close to Vijayawada and is inhabited by native tribes. Visit the local museum to learn more about the area's fascinating history.

Address: Araku Valley, Andhra Pradesh 531151

Timings: 09:00 a.m. to 08:00 p.m

Lambasingi Hills

One of the most beautiful hills close to Vijayawada is Lambasingi. Due to the cool weather, enormous waterfalls, and pepper plants, the location is full of peace. You must visit this place while exploring the places of Vijayawada.

Address: Lambasingi Hills, Andhra Pradesh

Timings: 24 Hours
