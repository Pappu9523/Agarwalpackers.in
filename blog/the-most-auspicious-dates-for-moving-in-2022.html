The Most Auspicious Day for New House Shifting in 2022

Moving to a new house is a huge milestone for most people. Whether it is a rented apartment or your own abode, moving to a whole new place can be exciting. This would be a start to a whole new chapter and you would want everything to be perfect on your moving day. While all the logistics will be taken care of by your packers and movers. There are a few things that you need to consider to ensure a hassle-free and peaceful move.

Your home does not merely offer a place to rest; it plays a big role in your day-to-day life and can even be the reason behind your happiness, prosperity, and good luck. Thus, it is important to pick an auspicious day to move your house or for your relocation and housewarming ceremony to ensure that you lead a better life ahead in your new abode.

But before starting with which day is good for house shifting in 2022, let us tell you more about the New House Shifting ceremony and how it is significant.

Significance of New House Shifting

The ceremony of New House Shifting is carried out before a family moves into their new home. The term ‘Griha Pravesh’ is made up of two words – Griha, meaning house, and Pravesh, meaning entrance. Thus, this ritual is performed to rid of all Vastu dosha and ill effects that may be present in the house so that you are able to start a new life in your new abode without any worries. Other than this, this ceremony helps in purifying the air inside the home, removes negative energy, and improves the level of positivity in the house.

The ceremony is typically carried out with the help of a priest while the family members need to be in attendance in order to carry out the necessary rituals and seek blessings.

New House Shifting is a one-time ceremony which makes it all the more important. This is the reason why you need to pick the correct date to perform this ceremony. Also, it is better to plan all the details for the ceremony well in advance and be prepared for everything.

Now that you know the importance of the house shifting ceremony, let us have a look at the most auspicious dates in 2022 that will prove to be best for your relocation and New House Shifting ceremony.

The Hindu Calendar

People who follow Hinduism should consider picking a lucky date to move into a new house in 2022 from the Hindu calendar for their relocation and know the good day for your housewarming ceremony. Let us know more about the months, days, and dates that are considered auspicious according to the Hindu calendar:

Month

Based on the Hindu Calendar, there are some particular months that are considered to be auspicious for moving into a new house. Typically, months like Magha, Phalguna, Vaisakha, and Jyeshtha are some of the most auspicious and lucky months for hosting a housewarming ceremony. Thus, moving during these months can prove to bring good luck, prosperity, and happiness into your lives. However, there are also some months, such as – Ashada, Bhadra, Ashvin, Shravan, and Pausha, during which moving into a new house is considered inauspicious. So, it is better to avoid entering your new home during these times.

Day

Just like auspicious months, there are also auspicious days for carrying out certain ceremonies. Based on the Hindu Calendar, you should plan your move on days – Dwitya, Panchami, Tritya, Saptami, Dashami, Ekadashi, and Trayodashi as these are considered to be auspicious days. However, some days that one should avoid are Purnima and Amavasya.

Other than this, there are some days that are considered to be lucky based on the English Calendar. For instance, Thursday is considered to be a good day for entering your new home. And on the other hand, days like Friday, Monday, and Wednesday are good for carrying out housewarming ceremonies. Thus, you can book your relocation services in a way that allows you to move into your new home on Thursday and you can simply settle down and prepare for your housewarming ceremony later.

Do's And Don'ts to remember before get into your new home

Griha pravesh is a vital puja according to the Hindu religion which is typically embraced to guarantee that the house stays liberated from all sorts of bad energy. It is likewise finished to look for the gifts of the God's so they can look after the house and favor it with favorable luck and shield it from the expectations of criminals.

Following are the Griha Pravesh Do’s and Don’ts which you have to keep in mind before moving into your new house.

  • 1. Before you start your griha pravesh ensure that the house is finished with the rooftop, entryways and windows
  • 2. Shift no furniture inside the house aside from the gas oven except if the Vastu Puja is finished
  • 3. Before you go into the house, break a coconut, which means the expulsion of the obstructions.
  • 4. While going into the house generally put your right foot first which is an indication of flourishing.
  • 5. The icons ought to be set in the east-bound heading of the house
  • 6. Milk ought to be bubbled which again is an indication of flourishing and conch shells ought to be brushed to avoid underhanded spirits.
  • 7. the house ought not be left unfilled for something like three days after the puja is finished, bombing which would invalidate the impacts of the puja
  • 8. On the off chance that the woman of the house is pregnant or a passing has occurred as of late in the family, then don't hold the puja.
  • 9. Remember to offer the minister with an adequate number of gifts and food things.
  • 10. Sprinkle the sacred water from the kalash with the assistance of the mango and go out sanitized.
