Top 7 Questions to Ask When Hiring Packers And Movers In Gachibowli

Packing up a home or office can be a time-consuming and tedious job. You have to pack all the delicate items carefully to make sure that they do not get damaged. Then, you will have to label the boxes correctly and keep track of all the things so that they do not get misplaced during the move. There are so many things you have to worry about while moving your home or office. Let’s not add packing and moving to that list and just rely on the services of reliable, local packers and movers.

But, wait: does that mean you can hire any nearby packers and movers you get and be done with it? Certainly, not! To ensure the safety and timely transit of your goods, you will have to hire the right packers and movers in Gachibowli. Here is a list of questions that you can ask a representative of the packers and movers Gachibowli company to make sure that you have made the right choice.

1. What kind of experience does the company have?

This is an important question if you are hiring services for unique or valuable items that many packers and movers usually do not need to deal with, such as fine art, piano, or antique furniture. For instance, if you hire a company like Agarwal Packers and Movers with over 37 years of experience, you can be assured that they can pack and move any of your valuable goods. Be it your expensive bike or your prized piano, Agarwal Movers have the experience, expertise, and tools to get the job done. And this experience comes in especially handy when you have planned a major shift, such as shifting your office or moving to a new city.

2. What is included in the company’s quote?

You have to be clear about what is included in the quote before you sign on the dotted line. Does the quote include insurance and storage, or is this something extra that you want the company to take into account? Check if packing is included in the quote. Also, ask freely about what extras come included in this quote.

There are times when the moving consultant tells you about so many facilities they provide and you feel these are automatically included in the quote. However, certain things will only be included if you ask for them. Be clear about what you are paying for, so that you are not bombarded with hidden charges later.

3. Does the company offer pack facilities?

Understandably, many people do not have enough time on their hands to pack all their belongings into boxes. Also, it would be wrong of you to assume that every company that offers moving services also includes packing services. So, find out if the moving company you have selected offers packing services as well, and take that worry off your shoulders.

4. Will the company protect all your valuables?

Ask the company how they plan on protecting your valuable belongings. Some of the things that you want to move might be priceless, and it will give you the peace of mind to know how the company is taking care of your things.

5. How will the company protect your home?

An important question to ask the company is how they plan to protect your home from the damages that might occur during the delivery or pick up of your furniture. They will have to make sure that both your house and the valuables they are handling remain safe during the process.

6. How long is the transit time?

The transit time might not be important for many of you, but some of you will plan your arrival around the time of delivery, especially if you are moving intercity. So, it is better to ask the company beforehand, and the best would be to get the link to track the movement of the vehicle carrying your goods. Nowadays, top companies have GPS in their trucks which means you can keep an eye on the movement of the vehicle in real-time.

7. What about the insurance?

Mishaps can happen even after all the precautions that you and your packers and movers take. So, you can insure your belongings beforehand and safeguard them. If the packers and movers offer insurance, ask them about the cover and if that is included in their quote. Also, you have the option of insuring some of your things beforehand anyway, such as a priceless painting or maybe some heirloom left by your grandparents.

The endnote

Asking these seven questions will make the move a lot easier on you. Also, are you looking for experienced and reliable packers and movers in your area? Search no more and contact Agarwal Packers & Movers today to find the answers to all your questions.
