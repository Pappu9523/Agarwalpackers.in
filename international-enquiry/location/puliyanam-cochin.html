Agarwal Packers and Movers Puliyanam

Puliyanam is a place located near Angamaly, that falls under Ernakulam district Aluva Taluk in the state of Kerala, India. It was once known for a forest with a large number of "Puli '' trees (Tamarind trees- Tamarindus indica) called puli forest and hence was called 'pulivanam' which later evolved as 'Puliyanam'. It is located 5 kilometres west of Angamaly and is located 11 km north of Cochin International Airport. The nearest railway station situated in Puliyanam in Angamaly.

Climate

As Puliyanam falls under Cochin district, its climate is also like Cochin district. This place has a very tropical climate and is classified as Am in the Kppen-Geiger classification by the scientists. The average temperature of this place is 26.6 C | 79.9F. It is situated 66 kilometres north of the state capital Trivandrum. The city is on the banks of Ashtamudi Lake and the Kallada river.

How to reach

Lonavala hill station is well-connected with other cities by road, by air, by train. Lonavala is situated between Pune and Mumbai. It is easy to reach Lonavala through these two cities.

By Air
The two nearest airports to Puliyanam are Trivandrum and Cochin. You can fly to these two airports and then take a cab or a taxi to Puliyanam. Trivandrum International Airport (IATA: TRV, ICAO: VOTV) is an international airport that mainly serves the city of Thiruvananthapuram in the Indian state of Kerala, India. It was established in 1932, it is also the first airport in the state of Kerala. It is also the fifth international airport of India officially declared by then Prime Minister of India, V. P. Singh.

By Rail
There is a railway station in Puliyanam. Many trains arrive here and people of Puliyanam transport from different areas to Puliyanam. Some of the names of the trains that come here are Trivandrum express and Amritha express. You can easily reach Cochin and then take a taxi or cab to Puliyanam.

By Road
Cochin is a big city. You can easily get to Cochin as it is well connected to all the different parts of the country. Thamarachal is well connected to Cochin via bus services. You can go to Thamarachal by one of the major roads known as NH544. This road is 44.8 kilometres long and can get you to Puliyanam.

Nearest Branch

The closest branch to Puliyanam is in Cochin. Since it's not very far from Puliyanam, customers find it easy and comfortable calling them and getting their work done.
