Agarwal Packers and Movers Mallapur

Hyderabad is the capital of southern India's Telangana state. Hyderabad is known for its technology industry and is one of the fastest developing cities in Southern India. Hyderabad has an age-old history and has been the capital of the Nizams for years. It is famous for the Char Minar and Film City. Mallapur is a locality in the Medchal- Malkajgiri district of Hyderabad. The area has all the basic amenities such as schools, hospitals, banks, shopping complexes, etc., and is well connected to different areas across Hyderabad.

Climate

The climate is a tropical wet and dry climate, close to a semi-arid climate as well. The highest average temperature is 39.5° C during May and in winters the lowest average temperature is 16.9° C in December. The average precipitation during the year is 607 mm and the main source of rain is the South-West monsoon.

How to Reach

By Air

Being one of the most industrially active cities, Hyderabad is well connected to the rest of India by airways and several airlines operate in different directions connecting Hyderabad to different places across the country. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad is the nearest airport and is located at a distance of 36.9 km from Mallapur. It connects Mallapur to the rest of India as well as to cities across the world. From the airport, one can rent a cab or take a bus and reach Mallapur in about 45 minutes.


By Rail

Hyderabad is connected to adjoining areas as well as cities and states across the country by a well-defined railway route. The nearest railway station is the NGRI Metro Station of the Hyderabad Multi-Model Transport system which is a suburban railway network, located at a distance of 6.4 km connecting Mallapur to different areas in Hyderabad as well as adjoining areas such as M.G Road, Begumpet, Gandhi Bhawan, Falaknuma, etc. Secundrabad Junction Railway Station is a major station located at a distance of 8.3 km and connects Mallapur to the different places across India such as Jaipur, Tirupati, Chennai, Delhi, Mumbai, etc. One can get an auto, cab, local train, or bus from these stations and reach Mallapur quickly.


By Road

Hyderabad is an important city in Southern India and is well connected to other cities across the country by a well-connected network of road and road transportation. Nacharam- Mallapur Road, and KrishnaDevaRaya Road are the major roads connecting Mallapur to Hyderabad and its adjoining areas, The TSRTC buses run across the city which connects Mallapur to the adjoining areas in Hyderabad. To reach Mallapur, one can take the state or private bus services, cabs, or self-drives from different cities and states to Hyderabad.

Closest Branch

The closest branch is situated at a distance of 13 km from Mallapur, covered in about 40 minutes through well-connected roadways. The proximity makes it easier for the customers to use the services.
