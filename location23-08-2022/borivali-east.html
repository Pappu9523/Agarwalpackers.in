Agarwal Packers and Movers Borivali East

Borivali East is a highly upper-class suburb area of Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is a part of the Borivali Tehsil and is located at the northwestern end of Mumbai. Sanjay Gandhi National Park, situated in Borivali, helps it retain its greenery and attract more tourists.

About Kalwa

The recently recorded status of real estate statistics shows Borivali as the primary residential zone of Mumbai, thus preferred by the superior property seekers.

Borivali East has every essential amenity except for an airport. The population of Borivali East as per the 2011 census was 13,000, which has now grown larger. Even after being urbanized, Borivali Municipal is still nurturing prehistoric era's Mandapeshwar Cave, Kanheri Cave, and Sri Swayambhu Ganpati Devalaya to preserve its history.

Climate

The main seasons in Borivali East are Summer, Monsoon, and Winter. The area receives 1/3rd of the total rainfall in Mumbai, which was 3.670.4mm in the previous year. The winters are a little bit cold, with the temperature falling to an average of 16°C. This temperature rises to an average of 35°C in summers.

How to Reach


By Air

The Mumbai Airport (BOM), which is said to be the busiest, is the nearest to Borivali East. It hosts all the domestic flights from Mumbai to nearby cities. Available options to reach Borivali from the Airport are bus, train, or cab.
The Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport is approximately 17kms away from Borivali.


By Rail

The Borivali Railway Station being 10 minutes walk away from Borivali East, has weekly and daily trains from cities all over India. The station is highly secured and has all the basic amenities for people from all backgrounds. The bus station of Borivali is attached to the railway station to avoid any inconveniences to the passengers.


By Road

Borivali East has the availability of both local as well as long routes bus transport. Being 10 minutes walk away, the Borivali Bus Stand is the nearest bus stand to Borivali East. The buses go to the nearby areas, which include Churchgate, Goregaon, Mawal, and Chandivali, to name a few.

The buses maintained by the State Government connect Borivali to other nearby states like Karnataka, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Goa, Gujarat, and Tamil Nadu.


Closest Branch

The nearest branch of Agarwal Packers and Movers is approximately 17 km away and takes around 31 minutes to reach Borivali East. The proximity makes customers call in to use their services.
