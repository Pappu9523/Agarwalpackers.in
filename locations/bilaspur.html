Bilaspur is an ancient city in the state of Chhattisgarh located in central India. It is one of the most prominent locales in the state, carrying a rich cultural heritage. It also is recognized as the electric power generation hub of the nation. Renowned for its top-quality rice and Kosa silk sarees, this city comes under the 100 cities that have to be transformed into smart cities under Smart Cities Mission.

About Bilaspur

Bilaspur is located on the northern side of Chhattisgarh alongside the Arpa River. This city was named after Bilasa, who was a fisherwoman residing here during the 17th century. During this period, this place was inhabited only by fishermen. Today, this city has a robust social infrastructure with many vital universities, colleges, and reputed hospitals. It also provides for the banking and financial needs of the residents. As this city has existed since olden times, it has many ancient temples which attract history enthusiasts. People of this region have preserved their cultural heritage and embrace it even today with full enthusiasm. Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary, located near this area, comprises diverse flora and fauna, which catches the tourist's eye.

Climate

This region is blessed with a mild and pleasant winter season. However, the summer season is not easy. This hot weather season is characterized by sweltering heat, with temperatures escalating to almost 45 degrees. The monsoon season in Bilaspur begins in late June and provides relief from the heated summers.

How to Reach

If you want to reach Bilaspur from Bhilai, you can drive in 2 hours and 42 minutes, covering a stretch of 144.5 km. A train journey is also very convenient as railway junctions serve both cities. You can take a train from Durg Junction in Bhilai to Bilaspur Junction.


Transportation

Bilaspur Railway junction is one of the busiest junctions in India. This junction connects Bilaspur to almost every city in the country. Bilaspur has excellent air connectivity as in the southern part of the city Bilaspur airport is situated. The international airport of Raipur is 133.5 km from here. Several national highways passing through this locale have provided road connectivity to this area.
