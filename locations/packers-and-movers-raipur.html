About Raipur

Raipur has been in existence since the 9th century, as pointed out by the archaeological pieces of evidence found from the remains of forts and some ancient sites. This city formed a part of the Maurya dynasty and the Dakshina Kosala Kingdom. While this city served as the Haihaya Dynasty's capital, it built several forts here. Today this place is a key industrial and business hub with more than 200 steel mills and six steel plants. The city also hosts many other industries like coal, aluminum, and power.
